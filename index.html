About me

Hi, my name is Dieter.


I’ve been living in Kiel for 14 years. After graduating in geosciences (B.Sc.) I recently completed my master’s degree in geophysics (M.Sc.). During the last few semesters, I discovered my passion for software development — especially web development. I find it exciting to build things that connect across layers and systems, using net-connected resources and ideally doing it all from a remote setup. Python is the language I’m currently most fluent in. On the personal side, I’m a single father (my wife is working abroard) and have two amazing kids at home.

Skills and Technologies

Python

I am passionate about writing Python scripts and programs to analyze, explore and process large-scale data in a scientific environment. Python is the programming language I'm currently most fluent in.

HTML & CSS

I use HTML5 and CSS5 to build clean, responsive web pages. I feel comfortable with turning design concepts into functional layouts.

JavaScript & PHP

As an active participant in the OpenCampus WebDev program, I work with JavaScript and expand my PHP skills.

(Radar-)Remote Sensing & GIS

My focus is on analyzing large volumes of time series data from radar remote sensing and presenting both the data and results using GIS applications.

Geophysics

I hold a master’s degree in geophysics, the science of measuring physical properties to understand the Earth’s dynamics from its surface to its deep interior. My expertise lies in satellite geophysics and hydroacoustics.

Geosciences

As a geoscientist with a bachelor’s degree, I focus mainly on petrology and near-surface geophysics, combining physics and geoscience to gain a deeper understanding of the geological processes that shape our daily lives.
